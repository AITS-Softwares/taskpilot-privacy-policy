Privacy Policy

TaskPilot by AITS Softwares

Effective Date

Introduction

TaskPilot is a task management application developed by AITS Softwares. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles user information.

Information We Collect

TaskPilot does not require users to create an account.

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No financial information is collected.
  • No location information is collected.
  • No contacts are accessed.
  • No photos or media are uploaded to our servers.

Offline Usage

The application is designed to work both online and offline. Tasks created by users are stored locally on the device unless synchronization features are explicitly added in future updates.

Data Storage

Your task information remains on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store your task data.

Third-Party Services

The application does not intentionally share user data with third-party services. If future versions integrate Google services such as Firebase, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Permissions

TaskPilot only requests permissions necessary for normal application functionality. Requested permissions are never used to collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

TaskPilot is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
